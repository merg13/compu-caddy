Golf Metrics That Matter

Track your performance with industry-standard calculations that reveal your true strengths and areas for improvement

USGA Handicap

Your official handicap index, calculated using the latest USGA formula to ensure fair and accurate course ratings.

Strokes Gained

Measure your performance against the field by analyzing how many strokes you gain or lose on each aspect of your game.

Greens in Regulation (GIR)

Track how often you hit the green in the regulation number of strokes, revealing your approach shot accuracy.

Scrambling

Calculate your success rate when missing the green, showing how well you recover from approach shot misses.

Sand Saves

Monitor your bunker play effectiveness by tracking how often you get up-and-down from sand traps.

One-Putt Percentage

Analyze your putting from inside 10 feet, highlighting your short game consistency and pressure performance.

Birdie Average

Track your scoring prowess by measuring how often you make birdies, indicating offensive capabilities.

Bogey Avoidance

Monitor how well you avoid costly mistakes, focusing on consistency and damage control in your rounds.

Install Compu-Caddy

Works on all your devices - PC, iOS, and Android

Desktop

Windows, Mac, Linux

  1. Visit this page on your computer
  2. Look for the install icon in your browser's address bar
  3. Click "Install" or "Add to Desktop"
  4. Launch from your applications menu

iOS (iPhone/iPad)

Safari browser required

  1. Visit the app URL (https://merg13.github.io/compu-caddy/app/) in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" to confirm

Android

Chrome, Edge, or Firefox

  1. Open this page in your browser
  2. Look for the "Install App" banner or menu option
  3. Tap "Install" or "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Find Compu-Caddy in your app drawer

Or install it later using your browser's install option
